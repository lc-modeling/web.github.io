Programs

Pageantry

Shape, groom, and prepare girls and women into confident models and beauty queens.

Our beauty Pageantry shapes, grooms, and prepares girls and women into gorgeous, confident, and promising models, beauty queens, and divers.

We build our students' confidence, leadership skills, and mold them to become good role models to society, by helping them come up with impactful projects that contribute to the growth of society.

For children pageantry, we empower them to be confident in front of a large crowd by giving them a pageantry experience through the Miss L&C competition that happens annually. The class is open to every girl and woman aged 12 and above.

  • Art of Communication: Essential interview skills, speech enhancement, mic utility, etc.
  • Ramp Walk: Posture, pose, stage presence, etc.
  • Fitness & Diet: Diet/Nutrition, exercise routine.
  • Camera Facing Technique & Portfolio Assistance: Modeling techniques, practice photoshoot session.
  • Makeup Lessons: How to do makeup for various assignments.
  • Table Etiquette: Eating style, table setting, napkin etiquette.
  • Social Graces: Social etiquette, personal hygiene.
  • Power Dressing Lessons: Styling tips for your body type, accessorizing.
  • Pageantry Q&A Preparation: Question handling, personalizing answers.

Runway Modeling

Learn to walk confidently on the runway and perfect your modeling skills.

A first show starts with a great walk. Our runway classes offer a positive mindset and wellness as the foundation of the models. Students have a fundamental skill to learn with passion and persistence, allowing them to reach their full potential.

  • How to walk confidently on the runway
  • How to perfect your posture
  • How to make great portfolio pictures
  • How to pose on the runway
  • How to control your facial expressions
  • How to make a great facial impression at castings

Commercial Modeling

Break into the modeling world regardless of your body type.

Commercial modeling embraces all looks, ages, and body shapes for advertising, campaign, and product marketing. It is a rewarding field within the fashion industry that offers individuals an opportunity to be the face of a product and connect with consumers on a visual emotional level.

  • How to build your portfolio
  • How to create a presence on social media
  • Fitness and diet
  • Camera facing technique

Etiquette Classes

Learn essential etiquette skills for any social context.

Etiquette is a life skill and not a complicated rule. Our etiquette classes teach you to apply etiquette to any social context, equipping you with the skills to communicate appropriately in any medium. Our straightforward and practical programs will make your future interactions with others more effective and enjoyable.

  • Dining etiquette
  • Image consultancy, including style and beauty
  • Elegant social skills
  • Graceful movement: sitting, standing, and walking
  • How to gain confidence in any social situation
  • Conversation etiquette at any social events
  • Mastering effective eye contact
  • Social media, email, and phone manners
  • How to deal with bad manners
